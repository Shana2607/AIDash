About the Recycling Drive

Join our Community Recycling Drive and be a part of our efforts to promote environmental awareness and recycling practices in our community. Recycling helps reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect our environment for future generations.

How to Participate

Participating in the recycling drive is easy:

  1. Collect recyclable materials such as paper, cardboard, plastics, glass, and aluminum cans.
  2. Bring your recyclables to our designated collection center on the specified date and time.
  3. Our team will assist you in sorting and processing the recyclables for proper recycling.
